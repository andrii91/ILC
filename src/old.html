company overview

Our main mission is to provide complex logistic and transportation services for dry bulk cargoes which are tailored to the requirements of our clients and the specific of each cargo.

ILC INTERNATIONAL LOGISTIC COMPANY LTD offers integrated logistics on a turnkey basis where bulk cargoes dispatched by one mean of transport are further transshipped to another for delivery to the destinations all over the world.

We involve road, rail, and sea transport to build effective supply chain with the optimal combination of freight forwarding rate and transit time. In cooperation with major shipping lines, we perform containerized cargo shipments.

Our logistic solutions are worldwide, but we are experts in transportation of dry bulk cargoes originated from Commonwealth Independent States countries.

More than 5 million tonnes of cargoes are transported by ILC annually.
